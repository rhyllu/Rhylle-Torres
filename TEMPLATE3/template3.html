I LOVE FISH!

FISHIES!

Beneath the waves where shadows play, In realms of blue, where sunbeams stray, A world of wonders, vivid and free, Where fish glide with grace, in the endless sea. With scales that shimmer, like jewels aglow, They dance in currents, swift or slow. In schools they roam, a living stream, In colors bold, like a vibrant dream.

FUN FACTS!

Colorful Characters: Some fish, like mandarinfish, sport vibrant hues.

Electric Eels: They zap prey and foes with electric shocks.

Salmon Journey: Pacific salmon travel thousands of miles to spawn.

Mimic Masters: The mimic octopus impersonates various creatures.

Ageless Sharks: Greenland sharks can live for over 400 years.

Seahorse Dads: Male seahorses carry and birth their young.

Fish are cool, and so are you! <3
